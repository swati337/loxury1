Privacy & Policy

At Miyabi Jewels, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe online experience. By using our website, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described in this policy. This information is securely stored and used solely for transaction purposes, customer service, and enhancing your shopping experience. By using our website, you consent to the collection and use of your personal information as described in this policy. For any questions or concerns about your privacy, feel free to contact us.
